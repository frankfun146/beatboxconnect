About Me

Hi, I'm Frank – a college student from California and a passionate beatboxer. Like many, I’ve found it challenging to connect with fellow beatboxers in the U.S. Inspired by the saying "be the change you want to see," I created BeatboxConnect to unite our community. Here, you can:

  • Explore tutorials featuring popular beatboxing sounds.
  • Stay informed about upcoming beatboxing events across the country.
  • Engage in discussions and share your passion with others.

Although I currently manage the site on my own, I’m committed to enhancing it over time. If you’d like to collaborate, hang out, or even jam together, please reach out via our contact form. I look forward to connecting with you!
